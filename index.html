The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society. The Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the twin states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of 'useful learning'.

The College has:

  • • 70 spacious classrooms and 31 labs (all classrooms and labs are equipped with LCD projectors)
  • • Air-conditioned conference rooms
  • • An independent three-storied Library building
  • • An independent four-storied Student Centre (with canteen, gym, indoor sports, medical centre, placement cell)
  • • Auditorium (seating capacity: 1500, fully air-conditioned, equipped with WiFi and 4 high powered LCD projectors)
  • • Hostel
